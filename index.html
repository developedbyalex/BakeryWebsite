About Us

Welcome to Sweet Delights Bakery, a haven for bread lovers and sweet-toothed enthusiasts since 1995. Our passion for baking has made us a beloved destination in the heart of Yorkshire. Founded by the Thompson family, our bakery began as a small operation selling homemade bread and pastries. Over nearly three decades, we've perfected our craft, using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients to create mouth-watering treats that bring joy to our community every day.
